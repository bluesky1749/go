Where can I find more info on ETHER?

Official sources of information for Ether are the founders Twitter accounts Viii & CV, Ether Twitter account , Announcements in the Discord , and the official Ether website (Coming in Q1 2023).

What Discord role represents a WL?

There is no WL role. Roles E-1, E-2, E-3, Server Booster, Early Adventurer, and Noble Stone do not indicate that the user has a WL.

Can someone get multiple WL?

Each wallet can stack up to 3 WLs, equating to minting 3x Ether NFTs during the WL phase. If someone manages to obtain >3 WLs, @SpiritEther will reach out via DMs for a second wallet.
(1x whitelist = 1x mint)
